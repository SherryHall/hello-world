Thoughts on the Web

At the start, the web was considered just a toy for computer geeks. Most people did not see it as something that would have any relevance to them and their lives.

Now, the web is an essential part of most people lives. People who were once isolated due to age or health have a means to connect and socialize, greatly increasing their quality of life. In the future, employees will no longer have assigned desks. They will simply log in where ever they are, and their information and resources will come to them, resulting in significant savings.
